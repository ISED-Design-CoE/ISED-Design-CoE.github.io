Do you want to incorporate with one name or two names?

Select if you are incorporating your corporation under one name or under both official languages.

Please choose one option.

  • A combined name has to describe your business in both languages and must include the legal element "Inc."" at the end of the name (e.g. Boulangerie René Bakery Inc.)
  • Learn more about having one name
  • If you are proposing a name in the other official language, you must ensure that both language forms are not so different from one another that they appear to be two different corporations
  • Learn more about having two names

Doing business in Quebec?

Quebec may require a French form of your corporate name. It is advisable to check the acceptability of the translation with the Office de la langue française.
