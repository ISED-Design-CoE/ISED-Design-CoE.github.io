Propose a corporate name

Your proposed name will need to be evaluated, in order to avoid conflicts with regulations and with similar names, before it can be approved for your use.

Please type in your proposed name below.

  • Tip: a business name usually has three parts

    Mandatory

    Distinctive part

    Word or words that make your name different from other businesses' names in the same line of work.

    Ex.
    • Imperial Oil Limited
    • J. Turner Holdings Inc
    • Go Travel Corp

    Optional

    Descriptive part

    Word or words that tell us what it is your business does.

    Ex.
    • Imperial Oil Limited
    • J. Turner Holdings Inc
    • Go Travel Corp

    Mandatory

    Legal element

    Must choose one of the following: Limitée, Limited, Incorporée, Incorporated, Societé par actions de régime fédéral, Corporation, Ltée, Ltd., Inc., S.A.R.F or Corp.

    Ex.
    • Imperial Oil Limited
    • J. Turner Holdings Inc.
    • Go Travel Corp.
