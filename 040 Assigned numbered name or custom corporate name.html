Assigned numbered name or custom corporate name?

You can choose from an assigned numbered name for your corporation or a custom word name.

Choose the naming structure that works for you.

  • An assigned numbered name has three parts: an assigned number, the word "Canada" and a legal element
  • Learn more about numbered corporate names
  • A custom corporate name is the legal name of a corporation
  • Requires that Corporations Canada evaluate if the proposed name might generate confusion with other incorporated businesses or trademarks
  • Learn more about custom corporate names
