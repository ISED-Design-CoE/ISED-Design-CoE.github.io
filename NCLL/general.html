Why are we asking this question?

Any entity, or affiliate of that entity (as defined in section 6.2 in SPB-001-23), with 100,000 or more retail mobile phone subscribers as reported to the CRTC through its Annual Telecommunications Survey is ineligible to hold a licence for the blocks from 3940 MHz to 3980 MHz.
