Bandwidth (required)
More information about bandwidth

You may request either 10MHz or 20MHz contiguous (i.e. adjacent frequency blocks).

If you require non-contiguous frequency blocks, please select 10MHz and then apply for a second licence.

You may request either between 50MHz and 400MHz contiguous (i.e. adjacent frequency blocks).

If you require non-contiguous frequency blocks, apply for a multiple licences.
Would you like ISED to assign you a frequency block? (required)
Choose your frequency blocks (required)
Channel AA1 and AA2 are available on a no-protection, no-interference basis to 27 GHz flexible use operations.

The NCL licensees with operations on block AA1 and AA2 shall not claim protection from nor cause interference to existing, modified or new flexible use systems in the 27 GHz band.

Channel A1 is available on a no-protection, no-interference basis to 3800 MHz flexible use operations

The NCLL licensees with operations on block A1 shall not claim protection from nor cause interference to existing, modified or new flexible use systems in the 3800 MHz band.

Licence type (required)
More information about licence type
  • A low-power licence can be up to 15 km² in area.
  • A medium-power licence can be between 75 and 165 km², but is not allowed in urban areas.

For further information about the consequences of this choice, including maximum permissible e.i.r.p. and maximum antenna height above ground level, see SRSP-521.

  • Indoor-only licences must be low power.
