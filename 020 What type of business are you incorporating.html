What type of business are you incorporating?

Choose the type of incorporation that is right for you.

Business
corporation

  • Is incorporated under the Canada Business Corporations Act
  • Business corporations issue shares and the owners are the shareholders
  • Basic incorporation: Quick and easy incorporation with pre-determined basic provisions for articles of incorporation, one class of common shares and an assigned numbered name
  • Custom incorporation: Gives you flexibility to tailor your corporate name, articles of incorporation and shares
  • Learn more about business corporations

Not-for-profit
corporation

  • Is incorporated under the Canada Not For Profit Corporations Act
  • Do not issue shares, and they have members
  • Learn more about not-for-profit corporations
