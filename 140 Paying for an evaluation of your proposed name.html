Paying for an evaluation of your proposed name

Once you complete your application, an examiner will evaluate your proposed name to eliminate any possible confusion with other registered businesses and trademarks. If there is any confusion, the examiner will ask you for more information through your email or phone number.

Please enter your payment information. All fields are required. We accept MasterCard, Visa and Visa Debit.

CAD $13.80 will be charged to your credit card for the evaluation of your name.
