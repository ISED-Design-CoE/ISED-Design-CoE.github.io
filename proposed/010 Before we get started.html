Is federal incorporation right for you?

  • Benefits of incorporating federally
    • The right to use your business name across the country
    • Limited liability
    • Lower corporate tax rates
    • Better access to capital and grants
    • Learn more about federal incorporation.
  • What you need to complete the process
    • $200 for a numbered corporation or $213.80 for a named corporation
    • 10 – 30 minutes
    • For online incorporation, payment is available only by credit card
    • Business shares information
    • Board of director information
    • Possible need of other documents such as: Name consents, proofs of registrations, etc.
    • Learn more about what you need to incorporate.
  • Other options if federal incorporation isn't for you
    • Provincial or territorial incorporation
    • Sole proprietorship or partnership
    • Cooperative
    • Learn more about alternatives to federal incorporation.
