Before we get started

Here's what you'll need:

  • For basic incorporation:
    • $200 and 10 minutes
  • For a numbered corporation:
    • $200
    • Information about your board of directors
    • Information about your share structure or membership structure
    • 15 minutes
  • For a named corporation:
    • $200 + $13.80
    • Information about your board of directors
    • Information about your share structure or membership structure
    • Proof of ownership or letters of consent for businesses with similar names
    • 30 minutes

We accept MasterCard, Visa and Visa Debit.

Information you provide will be kept confidential.
