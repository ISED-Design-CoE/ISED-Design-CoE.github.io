Distinctive words in your proposed names

To evaluate your names properly, we'll need to know the distinctive parts.

Parts of a corporate name

Mandatory

Distinctive part

Word or words that make your name distinct from other businesses in your industry.

Examples:
  • Imperial Oil Limited
  • J. Turner Holdings Inc
  • Go Travel Corp

Optional

Descriptive part

Word or words that tell us what your business does.

Examples:
  • Imperial Oil Limited
  • J. Turner Holdings Inc
  • Go Travel Corp

Mandatory

Legal element

Inc. etc.

Examples:
  • Imperial Oil Limited
  • J. Turner Holdings Inc.
  • Go Travel Corp.
