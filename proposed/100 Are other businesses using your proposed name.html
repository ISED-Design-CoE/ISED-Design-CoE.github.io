Similar names

Here's a list of other corporations' names, as well as trademarks, that are similar to the distinctive part of your name. There are always 35 items in this list.

The more similar your name is to these names, the more likely we are to reject it. We want Canadian companies to have names that are clearly different from each other.

If there is a reason we should not be concerned about a possible naming conflict with any of the names on this list, then please tell us about it. Check the box next to the name, and on the next screen you'll be able to upload documents to support your claim.

Some examples include:

  • When your own business shows up on this list, for instance because you've already registered your name with a province, or you've filed a trademark
  • When you have permission from the company listed, or from the owner of the trademark listed, to use a similar name
  • When the business in the list targets different customers from you, or operates in a different location

On the next screen we will ask you for supporting documentation.

You can also go back and enter a different name.
