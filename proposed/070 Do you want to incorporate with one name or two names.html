Do you want to incorporate with one name or two names?

Now it's time to tell us about the name you want. First of all, is there just one name (whether English, French, or bilingual), or do you have separate English and French versions?

Note that if you want to operate in Quebec, you'll need to have a French or bilingual name.

  • A bilingual name has to describe your business in both languages,e.g. "Boulangerie René Bakery Inc." It must include the legal element "Inc." at the end of the name
  • You can add a second language version of your name at any time
  • The two names must be translations of each other
  • If you'll be operating in Quebec, you may wish to check the acceptability of the translation with the Office de la langue française
