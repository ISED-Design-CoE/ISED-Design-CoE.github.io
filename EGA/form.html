Lifecycle phase (required)

Vision and strategy

Have overall goals and expected outcomes (including the intended impact on equity-deserving populations) and key performance indicators (KPIs) been defined that include how equity-deserving populations’ experiences will be measured versus the overall population?

For regulatory programs, if the GBA Plus assessment does not identify any considerations, include the following statement here: “No GBA Plus impacts have been identified for this proposal as noted in the TRIAGE and/or RIAS” and mark N/A on all other categories in this assessment if applicable.

Has the addressable population been defined and is the expected representation of equity-deserving groups within it understood?

Has an engagement strategy and plan to build relationships with equity-deserving groups been created? Are there other Departmental initiatives targeting the same groups, where a coordinated approach could lead to efficiencies and reduce the administrative burden on these groups?

Are potential barriers (and enablers) to access for equity-deserving groups understood and have risk mitigations to address barriers been developed?

Data

Has a data collection, storage, and use strategy and plan including quantitative factual data and qualitative experiential data and a privacy impact assessment been developed and explained to all stakeholders?

Are data collection tools accessible, do they enshrine consent, and do they clearly communicate how information that is collected will be used, especially with appropriate provisions made to protect personal information?

Execution parameters

Have Government of Canada accessibility design principles for programs and services been followed?

Were eligibility and selection criteria established considering equity-deserving group characteristics and been tested to identify unintended bias?

Do tailored supports for equity-deserving individuals exist to ensure understanding of application and/or intake processes, e.g., a help line or a designated contact for culturally sensitive communities?

Do contribution agreements with third party delivery partners define how they will cultivate an environment of trust, achieve expected EDI objectives, and explain how the organization will be expected to report on attainment of objectives to the Department, and have they agreed to the rules of engagement?
