Are other businesses using your proposed name?

Having a unique name might be important to your business. Review and decide if you want to submit your proposed name for evaluation or propose a new one.

Doing business in Quebec?

  • If you are planning to do business in Quebec you may require a French form of your corporate name
  • If selecting a French form for a corporate name for carrying on business in Quebec, it is advisable to check the acceptability of the translation with the Office de la langue française
