Are other businesses using your proposed name?

Having a unique name is important to your business.

Review and decide if you want to submit your proposed name for evaluation or propose a new one.

Here's a list of other corporations' names, as well as trademarks, that are similar to or sound like your proposed name. If there is a reason we should not be concerned with a similar name, let us know.

Check the box next to the name of the corporation or trademark if you have a relationship with it.

Examples of a relationship may include owning the corporation or trademark, or having permission to use the similar name. You'll also have an opportunity to upload supporting documents later in the process.

  • This page always shows 35 results
  • Information in expanded view is the same as in the modal windows of the current integrated process (see mockup), with the following changes:
    • For federal corporations (e.g. Between Us Publishing Inc above), "Company number" has been hyperlinked to the appropriate information in the Search for a Federal Corporation
    • "Jurisdiction code" has been expanded out into "Jurisdiction" (e.g. "ON" -> "Ontario"), for readability
    • "City" has been renamed "Head office", to distinguish it from where the company is doing business
    • "Business activity (NAICS)" includes not just the NAICS code but also a human-readable name for the code, as well as a hyperlink
      • The name has also been pluralised to "Business activities (NAICS)" if there is more than one activity
    • For trademarks, "Wares and services" has been removed because it can be quite long
