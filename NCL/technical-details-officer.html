Technical details

Bandwidth (required)
More information about bandwidth

You may request either 10MHz or 20MHz contiguous (i.e. adjacent frequency blocks).

If you require non-contiguous frequency blocks, please select 10MHz and then apply for a second licence.

Will any of your antennas be outdoors? (required)
Licence type (required)
More information about licence type
  • A low-power licence can be up to 15 km² in area.
  • A medium-power licence can be between 75 and 165 km², but is not allowed in urban areas.

For further information about the consequences of this choice, including maximum permissible e.i.r.p. and maximum antenna height above ground level, see SRSP-521.

  • Indoor-only licences must be low power.

Bypass coexistence check (ISED use only)

    By default all coexistence checks are performed. The spectrum caps check is performed in all options regardless of which coexistence checks are being bypassed. This field is only available for officers.
